## Goal
<!-- owner: plan · create; a wrong goal returns to plan, never edited in place -->

One pkgdown article holds all data-collection-platform import instructions —
Qualtrics survey file (.qsf), Qualtrics questions file (.txt), and REDCap
instrument ZIP — and every download page's Qualtrics and REDCap card links the
right section of it.

## Scope
<!-- owner: plan · create/amend-via-gate -->

**In:** a new article `vignettes/articles/import-instructions.Rmd` with three
anchored sections covering the three import flows for all instruments; the
maintainer-approved REDCap steps relocated into it; new Qualtrics QSF and TXT
steps verified against Qualtrics' official documentation; every download page's
Qualtrics *and* REDCap card repointed to the correct section; the per-generator
REDCap `@details` steps replaced by an `@seealso` to the article.

**Out:**
- Participant-facing administration instructions — untouched; this article is
  *upload/import workflow* only, so IP1 (instrument content) is not engaged.
- The overview instrument page (SR/BR/HSUM link-card page) → its own candidate
  row (lineage M21).
- Purging stale `vignettes/.quarto/_freeze/` → stays the M17-follow-up candidate.
- Building this as a CRAN vignette → decided as a pkgdown article (web-only),
  matching the download pages.
